About WasteManager

WasteManager was born from a shared passion for sustainability and technology. Our platform revolutionizes how restaurants and food businesses manage their surplus food, turning waste into opportunity while making a positive impact on communities and the environment.

Founded in 2023, we've grown from a small startup to a trusted partner for hundreds of restaurants across the country. Our mission is to bridge the gap between food waste and food need through smart technology and compassionate action.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste by 50% in the restaurant industry by 2030 through innovative technology that makes sustainable practices simple and rewarding.

Our Values

Sustainability, Innovation, Community Impact, and Transparency guide every decision we make and every feature we build into our platform.

Our Impact

Since our launch, WasteManager has helped restaurants divert over 500,000 pounds of food from landfills, resulting in the equivalent of 1.2 million meals donated to communities in need.

Our partners report an average 40% reduction in food waste within the first six months of using our platform, translating to significant cost savings and environmental benefits.
