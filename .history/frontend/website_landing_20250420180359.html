About Us

WasteManager was born from a simple yet powerful idea — to reduce food waste and redirect surplus to those in need. Founded by a group of passionate engineers and change-makers, our mission is to create a seamless solution for restaurants to handle waste responsibly. Every click on our platform brings us one step closer to a cleaner, more compassionate world.
